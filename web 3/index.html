Suummary

Adrienne Clarkson is a Hong-Kong Canadain journalist and stateswoman who once served as the 26th Governor General of Canada. She was appointed as governor general by Queen Elizabeth II herself in 1999 and was in office until 2005. She has made immense contribution to the cidizenships in Canada, mainly through the funding of The Insititude for Canadian Citizenship. The ICC is a national, non-profit organization that engages Canadians in citizenship through innovative programs. Throughout her life times, she had published several books regarding her opinions on citizenships. In 2006, she established the Clarkson Cup, which is the championship trophy for Women's hockey.
